Fast starting point: ask for a 3-point mini audit first, no obligation.

Most small businesses do not lose leads because they lack traffic.

They lose leads because the enquiry path has friction, first-response expectations are unclear, or follow-up is inconsistent once a prospect reaches out.

Friction at the point of enquiry

Confusing contact options, weak call-to-action placement, or mobile friction can reduce completed enquiries before anyone on your team can reply.

Unclear response expectations

If a prospect does not know what happens next or how quickly they will hear back, trust drops early.

Follow-up leakage

Even when a lead comes in, inconsistent handoff or missing follow-up can waste demand you already paid to attract.

What the audit covers

I review your public lead-capture flow and show where leads may be leaking before a human reply happens.

CTA clarity

  • How obvious the next step is
  • Whether contact paths compete with each other

Contact-path friction

  • Form length and usability
  • Mobile friction and dead ends

Trust near conversion

  • Signals that help a visitor feel safe enquiring
  • Context gaps near booking or contact pages

Response expectations

  • Whether the visitor knows what happens next
  • Likely follow-up leakage points visible from the funnel
