Where people usually lose the trail

The wallet list only exists in your head

That feels manageable until a bridge or exchange transfer shows up from an address you forgot to include.

Bridge history gets split in half

When the send side and receive side are not saved together, same-owner movement starts looking like mystery activity.

Rewards and airdrops arrive with no value note

Later you remember the token, not the receipt date, the quantity, or what price source made sense at the time.

LP events get dumped into a vague bucket

Adding liquidity, removing liquidity, and claiming rewards are different events. Leaving them unlabeled is how cleanup drifts.
