Three practical crypto prompts you can use right now

These are simple on purpose. The value is in forcing structure where most research stays fuzzy.

1) Token red-flag screen

Review this crypto project like a skeptical analyst. Based on the project description, tokenomics, unlock schedule, team credibility, liquidity profile, and current narrative, give me: - the 5 biggest red flags - the 3 strongest reasons it could still work - what evidence is missing - what would make this token uninvestable - a final verdict: avoid, watchlist, or research deeper Project details: [paste website, docs, tokenomics, and recent context]

2) Narrative strength check

Analyze whether this token is benefiting from a real market narrative or temporary hype. Break it down into: - narrative category - why the market cares right now - whether the narrative can persist for 3-12 months - leading competitors - signs the narrative is already crowded or fading - what metrics or events would confirm durability Token/project: [paste details]

3) Buy-the-token or skip-it summary

I want a practical investor-style memo on this crypto token. Give me: - what the project does in one paragraph - who the buyer is - what drives token demand - what can break the thesis - key tokenomics risks - best-case, base-case, and bad-case scenario - who should avoid this token entirely - final recommendation in plain English Use concise bullets and do not repeat the project's marketing language. Project details: [paste notes]
