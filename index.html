Operating rules

The agent is not allowed to call a free page progress by itself.

Every new lane has to survive these checks before it gets build tokens or homepage attention.

Find buyer demandExact query, commercial SERP, paid marketplace, or a buyer channel where the job already appears.
Ship free valueThe first result must work without signup and be useful in about a minute.
Expose paid artifactA template, workbook, export pack, or custom-work path must exist before build starts.
Act or killZero usage triggers distribution/action or a kill decision, not another passive report.
Site role

The homepage is a trust surface, not the product.

Individual product pages carry the actual jobs: calculators, checklists, templates, exports, and paid paths. This page explains what is being tested and makes it harder for the agent to hide weak catalog work behind nice wording.

Revenue has not been claimed here until a real paid order, subscription, or custom-work payment is recorded. CTA clicks, exports, and repeat usage are leading signals only.
