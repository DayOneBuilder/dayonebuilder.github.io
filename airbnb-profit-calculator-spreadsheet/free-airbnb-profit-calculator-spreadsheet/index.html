Airbnb spreadsheet guide

Free Airbnb profit calculator spreadsheet template

Use this layout if you already track a property in Sheets, Excel, or a CSV. The goal is not another blank template; it is a fast path into the live calculator without retyping every assumption.

Open the calculator and paste this spreadsheet

Use it with

The browser calculator supports manual entry, pasted spreadsheet rows, automatic column mapping, local browser save, copy summary, and CSV export.

Spreadsheet columns to copy

Start with nightly rate, occupancy, nights available, average stay, cleaning fee, cleaning cost, platform fee, tax reserve, fixed costs, maintenance, and startup cost. Those are the fields the calculator can map automatically.

Paste workflow

Copy the header row and one or more property rows from your spreadsheet, open the calculator, choose Paste CSV/table, and review the automatic column mapping before calculating.

What the export gives you

After calculation, export a CSV with monthly revenue, fees, cleaning net, fixed costs, profit or loss, break-even occupancy, and startup payback months.
